Jeffrey Migneault, PhD
Licensed Clinical Psychologist

Jeff Migneault is a clinical psychologist who has been providing individual psychotherapy in the Providence area for over 20 years. He received his doctorate in clinical psychology from the University of Rhode Island and completed his clinical internship at Beth Israel Hospital in Boston. Jeff does individual adult work, from young adults to seniors.


He uses an eclectic approach to therapy, drawing on his broad range of training, and works with his clients to find the best fit for each person. His approach draws on Psychodynamic, Family Systems, and Cognitive Behavioral therapies. He also incorporates Mindfulness techniques and ideas, drawing on both formal training and many years of personal experience with contemplative practices.


Jeff approaches the work as a collaborative endeavor. He works with adults to address a wide range of issues including anxiety, depression, relationship issues, difficulties with unhealthy habits or addictions, and life-stage issues. Although he acknowledges the importance of understanding past events and experiences, his goal is to make changes in the present that allow for a person to be more fully themselves and engaged with the people and world around them.
