Welcome to Broadway Psychotherapy

Broadway Psychotherapy (formerly Health Behavior Consultants, Inc.) is a two-person psychotherapy practice located on the second floor of a large Victorian in the West End neighborhood of Providence, Rhode Island. We have been practicing together for over ten years and were previously located at 347 Broadway.


Between us we see clients across all age groups with a large range of challenges, who find us in multiple ways. Issues may include interpersonal relationship or family difficulties, anxiety, depression, health problems, child or parenting struggles and other adjustments to the inevitable stresses of developmental and life transitions, with their attendant joys and losses.


Our practice reflects our core philosophy that when people seek the experience of sharing with and feeling heard by others, they are more likely to find their own path toward healing and positive change.
